about us

Step into a world where the flavors of Persia come alive. Inspired by the grandeur of the Achaemenid Empire and the timeless traditions of ancient Iran, our restaurant brings you dishes that carry the soul of a thousand years of history. From the aroma of saffron and fresh herbs to the beauty of Persian hospitality, every plate is a journey back to a land of kings, poets, and timeless feasts.
